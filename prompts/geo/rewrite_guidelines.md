# GEO Rewrite Guidelines

When rewriting KB articles for AI answerability, follow these rules:

## Structure
- Start every article with a clear H1 and 1-sentence summary of what it covers
- Use H2 for major sections, H3 for sub-steps
- Lead with the answer, then explain why/how
- Use numbered lists for procedures, bullet lists for options/examples

## Q&A Blocks
Add explicit Q&A blocks at the end of each article:
- Q: [Most common question about this topic]
- A: [Direct, complete answer in 1-3 sentences]
Cover at least 3 Q&A pairs per article.

## Language Patterns for AI Retrieval
- Use exact product names as Shopify uses them (e.g., "Shopify Payments", not "the payment processor")
- State eligibility criteria explicitly: "This feature is available on all Shopify plans"
- Avoid passive voice: "You can process refunds from Orders > [Order] > Refund"
- Avoid ambiguous references like "this feature" without naming it

## What to Avoid
- Jargon or internal terms not visible to merchants
- Conditional language without clear conditions
- Contradicting information from other articles
